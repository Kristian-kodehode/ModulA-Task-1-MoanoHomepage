About Moano

Moano is a sleepy lofi creature who one day decided to go out on a boat journey. Far out at sea he met a massive storm. Moano got knocked out and when he woke up he realized he was stranded on an uncharted magical island.

So now our brave creature Moano explores the island to get inspiration for his musical endeavours.

During his travels he have come across these portals which opens up to different dimensions and worlds. So far he have found 5 and entered 3 of them and they are all gateways to different worlds.
